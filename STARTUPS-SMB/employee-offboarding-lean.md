# Lean Employee Offboarding Policy

**Effective Date:** October 2025 
**Policy Owner:** HR & Security Lead  
**Target Audience:** All employees, managers, and contractors  
**Classification:** Internal Use

---

## Executive Summary

This Lean Employee Offboarding Policy ensures that when an employee or contractor leaves the company, all access is removed, company assets are recovered, and sensitive information is protected. The process is streamlined for startups and small teams, balancing security with operational efficiency.

---

## 1. Policy Purpose & Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

- Prevent unauthorized access after departure
- Protect company data, IP, and customer information
- Ensure timely recovery of company assets
- Meet legal and contractual obligations

### 1.2 Scope

This policy applies to:

- All employees, founders, and contractors
- All company systems, data, and devices
- Voluntary and involuntary departures

---

## 2. Offboarding Steps

### 2.1 Notification

- Manager or HR notifies IT/Security of departure date as soon as possible
- For involuntary departures, notify IT/Security before informing the employee

### 2.2 Access Removal

- Disable all user accounts (email, cloud, code repos, SaaS, VPN) on or before last day
- Remove access to shared drives, Slack, and other collaboration tools
- Revoke physical access (badges, keys, office entry)
- Remove MFA tokens and reset shared credentials if used

### 2.3 Asset Recovery

- Collect all company-owned devices (laptops, phones, tokens, storage)
- Retrieve or wipe company data from BYOD devices if permitted
- Recover any physical documents or storage media

### 2.4 Exit Interview & Reminders

- Remind departing staff of confidentiality and non-disclosure obligations
- Conduct a brief exit interview (optional for small teams)

### 2.5 Documentation

- Record all offboarding actions (access removed, assets returned)
- Retain documentation for audit and compliance

---

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities

| Role                | Responsibility                                 |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| HR                  | Coordinate offboarding, update records         |
| Manager             | Notify HR/IT, collect feedback, support process|
| IT/Security Lead    | Remove access, recover assets, document actions|
| Employee/Contractor | Return assets, comply with exit requirements   |

---

## 4. Quick Reference Checklist

- [ ] HR/Manager notified of departure
- [ ] IT/Security notified in advance
- [ ] All accounts and access disabled
- [ ] All company assets collected or wiped
- [ ] Confidentiality reminder given
- [ ] Offboarding actions documented
