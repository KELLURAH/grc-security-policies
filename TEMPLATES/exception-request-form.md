## 1. Exception Details

### 1.1 Policy/Control Reference
Identify the specific policy or control requirement from which an exception is being requested (e.g., *Access Control 4.4: MFA mandatory for all production environments*).

### 1.2 Description of Exception
Provide a detailed description of the requested exception. What specific requirement cannot be met?

### 1.3 Scope
Specify the systems, users, data types, or business units included in this exception.

### 1.4 Business Justification
Explain why this exception is necessary. Why can the standard control not be applied? (e.g., technical limitation, legacy system, third-party dependency).

---

## 2. Risk Assessment

### 2.1 Potential Risk
What are the potential security risks associated with not implementing the required control? (e.g., unauthorized access, data breach, loss of audit trail).

### 2.2 Mitigation / Compensating Controls
List any alternative controls or measures that will be put in place to reduce the risk during the exception period.
